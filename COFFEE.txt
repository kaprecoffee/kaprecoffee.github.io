Espresso-based drink designed to resemble coffee brewed in a drip filter, considered popular in the United States of America. (AMERICANO)

Perfectly steamed milk, rich espresso and a delicate topping of foamed milk. (latte)

Combines espresso with a small amount of steamed milk and a deep layer of foam for stronger coffee flavor.  (cappuccino)

Full flavored, concentrated form of coffee that is served in ''shots''. (espresso)

Espresso-based coffee that has an even mix of liquid milk and smooth velvet foam. (flat white) 

Made by pouring a double-shot of espresso or ristretto over hot water. It is more strongly flavored. (long black)

Cocktail consisting of hot coffe, Irish whiskey, sugar, and topped with thick cream. (irish coffee)

Cornerstone of Italian coffee culture, basically an espresso with a small amount of foamed milk on top. (macchiato)

Strong black coffe served in glass topped whit whipped cream, it comes with powder sugar served separately. (vienna)

Espresso shot (double) with a combination of either steamed milk and cocoa powder or chocolate milk and sometimes topped with whipped cream.  (mochaccino)

Italian coffee-based dessert witha scoop of vanilla gelato or ice cream topped or ''drowned'' with a shot of hot espresso. (affogato)
