THE TEAM

The Kapre, coined from the words kap(e) + re(search), are four students from De La Salle University who are simply intrigued by coffee, its effects, and its distinct flavors.
Our goal is to share with you the knowledge we have retrieved from our extensive research on coffee, from the very plant, extraction, and production, to the product that is enjoyed by many.

Andrea Suarez

Andrea Suarez is a coffee enthusiast. She grew up smelling the aroma of coffee having a parents who drinks it everyday. She believes, as a student, caffeine is often a necessity. It motivates one to get up and face another day.

Gia Abella

Gia Abella is a student from De La Salle University and much like the other students she is in love and is addicted to coffee. Which led to the creation of this website, and in it is everything you need to know about coffee.

Angela Yu

Angela Yu is a student who never gave much thought about coffee until she enrolled in De La Salle University. The workload led her to realize how much caffeine could help her stay awake and alert. Due to her frequent coffee consumption, she wanted to know more about it, specifically its roots. This led to research and creation of this site, along with three other like-minded individuals.

Joaquin Bautista

Honing his skills from a known technical school, Joaquin Bautista a student of DLSU has been slowly becoming coffee-dependent. Because of his background with programming, they gather to share their findings about Coffee.
